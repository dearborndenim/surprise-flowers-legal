SMS program terms.

How our text messaging program works, what kind of messages you'll receive, and how to stop them.

Program operator

Surprise Flowers Co.
hello@surpriseflowers.co

What you'll receive

By providing your mobile phone number to Surprise Flowers — at checkout (with the "Receive SMS updates" checkbox selected) or by texting our published number — you agree to receive transactional and marketing SMS messages, including:

  • A heads-up before each scheduled bouquet delivery, with the option to defer to your alternate delivery window by replying.
  • A tracking notification when each bouquet ships.
  • A delivery confirmation when each bouquet arrives.
  • A short feedback request the day after delivery.
  • A "change the water" reminder two days after delivery.
  • Replies from our customer service team if you reach out.

Sample message

Heads up: a Surprise Flowers bouquet is shipping to your home around Tuesday next week. Want to switch to your alternate window (later in the cycle)? Reply DEFER and we'll move it. Otherwise, you'll get a tracking link the day it ships. — Surprise Flowers

Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your delivery cadence — typically 3 to 5 messages per delivery cycle. For an annual subscription that's 3 to 5 messages per year; for a monthly subscription, roughly 36 to 60 per year.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply. Surprise Flowers does not charge for SMS — your wireless carrier may, depending on your plan.

Opting out

You can stop receiving messages at any time by replying STOP, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, or QUIT to any message. You will receive a single confirmation reply, and no further marketing or transactional SMS will be sent to you. To resume, reply START.

Reply HELP for help, or email hello@surpriseflowers.co.

Privacy

Your mobile information will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. We share data only with subprocessors that are required to deliver these messages (for example, our SMS provider Twilio).

For full details see our privacy policy and terms of service.

How customers opt in

Two ways:

  • At checkout. When subscribing on surpriseflowers.co, you'll see an SMS marketing checkbox alongside the standard order confirmation phone number field. Checking it constitutes opt-in. The checkbox is unchecked by default.
  • By texting us. Texting our published phone number with the keyword START (or just any first message) opts you in.

You can withdraw consent at any time using the opt-out steps above.
